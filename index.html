Work Plan &
Approach

Ground Review understands The Hub. as more than a venue — it is an urban living room for Downtown Corona. Our approach activates the space with intention: programming that serves residents, drives foot traffic for tenants, and establishes The Hub. as a regional destination.

Our Understanding of The Hub.
The Opportunity
The Hub. sits at the center of Corona's downtown revitalization story. With the newly adopted Entertainment Zone ordinance, an event-capable outdoor Backyard, multi-tenant F&B space, and proximity to residential neighborhoods — the ingredients for a thriving community hub are in place. What's needed is a disciplined, culturally-attuned operator to bring it to life consistently.
Key Challenges We've Identified
Consistency: One-off events don't build destinations — recurring, programmed rhythms do.

Tenant Symbiosis: Programming must drive traffic to tenants, not compete with them.

Diverse Audiences: Corona's community requires culturally diverse, multi-generational programming — not a one-size-fits-all calendar.

Value &
Compensation

Ground Review proposes a hybrid compensation model that aligns our incentives with The Hub.'s success — protecting the City's downside while rewarding performance through shared upside.

Option A
Flat Monthly Management Fee
A fixed monthly fee covering all programming, operations, marketing, and management services. Provides budget predictability for the City. Best suited if the City prefers full cost certainty throughout the initial term.
Preferred Model
Hybrid: Base Fee + Revenue Share
A modest base management fee combined with a percentage of event ticketing, vendor fees, and sponsorship revenue secured. This aligns Ground Review's financial incentives directly with The Hub.'s success — the more we grow, the more we earn. Transparent, performance-driven, and fiscally responsible for the City.
Ground Review's Recommendation
Option C
Performance-Based Incentive Structure
Base compensation tied to agreed performance metrics: attendance thresholds, sponsorship dollars secured, event frequency, and tenant sales impact. Milestone-based bonuses upon achievement. Maximum accountability for results.
Fee Proposal Note
Ground Review's detailed Labor Hour Estimate and Fee Proposal — including hourly rate schedule by classification, task-level labor hour breakdown, and total Maximum Not-to-Exceed fee — is submitted as a separate document per RFP Section V.9, uploaded to PlanetBids as the Cost File. All rates include printing, mileage, telephone, mailing, and incidental expenses. No additional charges will be assessed.
